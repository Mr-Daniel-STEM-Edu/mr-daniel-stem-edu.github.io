Chapter 1 Notes

Series (Constant $I$): $$R_{eq} = R_1 + R_2 + ... + R_n$$

Parallel (Constant $V$): $$\frac{1}{R_{eq}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + ... + \frac{1}{R_n}$$

Ohm's Law: $$V = I \cdot R$$

Power: $$P = V \cdot I = I^2 \cdot R = \frac{V^2}{R}$$

Terminal Voltage: $$V_{term} = V_B - I \cdot r$$
